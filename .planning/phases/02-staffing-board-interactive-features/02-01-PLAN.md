<objective>
Make assignment cards in project columns draggable with always-visible drag handles (grip icon), and extend the drag overlay to show appropriate preview for both employee and assignment drags. The original card stays in place at ~80% opacity during drag.

Purpose: This is the foundation for all Phase 2 drag operations. Without draggable assignment cards, users cannot drag-to-home (remove) or drag-to-project (copy). The always-visible grip handle makes the drag interaction discoverable per user's CONTEXT.md decision.

Output: Assignment cards with visible IconGripVertical handles, useDraggable wiring with type-tagged data, and a dual-type DragOverlay that distinguishes employee vs assignment drags.
</objective>
